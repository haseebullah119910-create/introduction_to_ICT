Introduction to Information & Communication Technology

Information and Communication Technology (ICT) refers to all technologies used to handle telecommunications, broadcast media, intelligent building management systems, audio-visual processing, and network-based control. ICT helps people communicate and share information quickly and easily. It plays a vital role in almost every field of life.

ICT includes tools that allow us to store, retrieve, transmit, and manipulate data. These technologies help businesses, schools, and hospitals work more efficiently. Modern ICT systems use computers, the internet, and smart devices to support digital activities. ICT has made communication faster and more reliable.

ICT also supports scientific work, such as writing chemical formulas like H2O or mathematical expressions like x2. These technologies help students learn, teachers teach, and professionals work more effectively in todayâ€™s digital world.

Steps to Use a Computer

  1. Connect the power cable
  2. Press the power button
  3. Wait for the system to start
  4. Login to your account
  5. Start using applications
